Fun & Games
Games

We have games for all tastes... games to help you practise your English and others just for fun. Play, enjoy and learn!
561
Beat the Keeper

Are you a football fan? Try this game to see how many football words you know in English. Can you beat the goalkeeper? Good luck!
981
Magic Gopher

Think of a number with two digits. For example 43. Follow the Magic Gopher's instructions and he will amaze you by telling you what number you chose.
642
Pic-your-wits

What's the picture? You have 20 seconds to beat the clock. The faster you guess the more you score. A really fun way to learn vocabulary!
559
Spelloween

Spelling is terrifying! Can you spell quickly or will your fear be too much?
1054
Verb Machine

You can make any English tense or verb form by clicking four buttons. Don't believe us? Have a go!
178
Word wrangling

Word Wangling is a collection of fun word games to help you look for patterns, shapes and relationships between letters and words.
2751
Wordshake

How many points can you get in 3 minutes? Longer words score more points!
